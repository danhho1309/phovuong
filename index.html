Welcome to Pho Vuong Duncan

281 Canada Ave #1, Duncan, BC, Canada V9L 1T6

Half the earth away, in the heart of the tranquil and friendly Greater Vancouver Island, Pho Vuong appeared, just like a simple and cozy street vendor filled with gifts from Nha Trang. More than just an ordinary restaurant, Pho Vuong is not only a bridge connecting people who are away from their mother land, but also is a cultural ambassador who delivers the refined arts of Vietnamese cuisine to the world and bring the world closer to Vietnamese culture.
